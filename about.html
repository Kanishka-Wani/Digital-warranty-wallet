About Us

Your Digital Warranty Companion

We believe that managing warranties shouldn't be a headache. WarrantyWallet was created to simplify the way you track and protect your valuable purchases.

Why WarrantyWallet?

Every year, millions of people lose track of their product warranties, missing out on free repairs and replacements. WarrantyWallet was built to solve this problem — a simple, secure, and beautiful way to store all your warranty information in one place.

Whether it's your smartphone, laptop, or home appliance, we make sure you never miss an expiry date again. With automatic reminders and a clean dashboard, staying on top of your warranties has never been easier.
