How Warranty Wallet works

Three easy steps to keep your warranties organised and accessible.

1. Add Product

Enter product name, brand, purchase date, warranty duration, and optionally upload a bill image.

2. Auto‑Save

Data is stored securely in your browser's LocalStorage. Expiry date is calculated automatically.

3. Track & Manage

View all products, expiry status, and receive notifications for warranties expiring soon.
